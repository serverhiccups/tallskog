Arrow Algorithm Steps
* Four control points - start the first and last and the beginning and end nodes
* Find the lowest point in the set of obstacles between the two nodes
* The two middle controls points will slide along the x-axis of this baseline
* ? ?The two middle points start on top of each other at this point
* Draw a ray between the starting point and the baseline at a 90deg (vertical) angle
* If this ray does not intersect with any obstacles, attempt to move the ray in a little (with angle) and try the test again
* Until you have the ray as a tight as possible
* Do the same on the other side.
* The two middle control points lie on the intersect of the two rays and the baseline
